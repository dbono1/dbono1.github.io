Idea

My Idea for the Midterm Project is to create a website that is dedicated to advertising Affinity Photo/Affinity Designer over Adobe Photoshop/Illustrator. (It's a one time payment of $50 each and you get the software forever instead of having to subscribe to Adobe... check it out if you do photo editing as a hobby, but this is an aside) BUT, there are going to be a few hidden clues on the website that lead to puzzles that have to be solved. There's going to be a "terminal" page where you can type in your answers and check to see if you got the right answer.
