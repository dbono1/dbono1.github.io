James Fridman & His Technique

Application

  • Fridman’s main artistic tool is photoshop and indesign. He uses the features of these softwares in a variety of ways. Here are some tools that he utilizes:
  • Cropping people or objects out of images
  • Cropping people or objects into images
  • Changing colors on people’s clothes
  • Changing backgrounds
  • Changing people’s outfits
  • Changing scenery of pictures
  • Adjusting blur
  • Adjusting body positions
